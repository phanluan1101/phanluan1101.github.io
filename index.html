Introduction

Thien-Luan Phan obtained his BSc in Physics and Electronics Engineering in 2019 and received his MSc in Biomedical Engineering in 2021. His research interests include non-invasive techniques in prognosis and diagnosis, biomedical electronics/instrumentation, assistive healthcare technology, and application of deep learning models in medical devices.
